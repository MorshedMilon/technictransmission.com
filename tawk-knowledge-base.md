**Article Title:** What is a transmission inspection?
A transmission inspection is a thorough check of your vehicle's transmission system to ensure everything is functioning correctly. Our technicians look for signs of wear, fluid leaks, shifting problems, and any other issues that could affect performance. We recommend regular inspections to catch problems early and avoid costly repairs.

---

**Article Title:** What is a transmission rebuild vs. full replacement?
- **Rebuild**: The existing transmission is disassembled, worn parts are replaced, and it is reassembled. This is often more cost-effective if the core unit is still in good condition.
- **Full Replacement**: The entire transmission unit is replaced with a new or remanufactured unit. This is recommended when the transmission is beyond repair.

Our technicians will assess your vehicle and recommend the best option for your situation.

---

**Article Title:** How do I know if my transmission needs repair?
Common signs your transmission may need attention:
- Difficulty shifting gears or gears slipping
- Unusual noises (grinding, whining, clunking) when shifting
- Delayed or rough gear engagement
- Transmission warning light on dashboard
- Burning smell or fluid leaks under your vehicle
- Vehicle won't move in drive or reverse

If you notice any of these symptoms, contact us immediately for a diagnosis.
