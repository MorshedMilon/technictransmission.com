# 1. Common Transmission Symptoms

## Transmission Slipping

Symptoms:

* Engine RPM rises but vehicle speed does not
* Delayed acceleration
* Gear changes unexpectedly

Possible Causes:

* Low transmission fluid
* Worn clutch packs
* Worn bands
* Faulty solenoids
* Valve body problems
* Torque converter issues

Recommended Action:

* Check fluid level and condition
* Perform diagnostic scan

---

## Hard Shifting

Symptoms:

* Bang or jerk during shifts
* Rough gear engagement

Possible Causes:

* Dirty fluid
* Faulty shift solenoid
* Valve body issues
* Transmission Control Module (TCM) problems

Recommended Action:

* Diagnostic scan
* Fluid inspection

---

## Delayed Engagement

Symptoms:

* Delay when shifting into Drive or Reverse

Possible Causes:

* Low fluid
* Internal seal leakage
* Worn clutch packs
* Pump issues

Recommended Action:

* Transmission inspection

---

## Transmission Overheating

Symptoms:

* Burning smell
* Warning light
* Reduced performance

Possible Causes:

* Low fluid
* Clogged cooler
* Heavy towing
* Internal friction

Recommended Action:

* Stop driving if severe
* Inspect cooling system

---

## Transmission Noise

Symptoms:

* Whining
* Grinding
* Humming

Possible Causes:

* Low fluid
* Worn bearings
* Planetary gear damage
* Pump failure

Recommended Action:

* Immediate inspection

---

Condition:

Color:

* Dark brown or black

Condition:

* Burnt smell

# 3. Common OBD-II Transmission Codes

## P0700

Description:
Transmission Control System Malfunction

Meaning:
General transmission fault stored in TCM.

---

Description:
Input/Turbine Speed Sensor Circuit Malfunction

Possible Causes:

* Bad speed sensor
* Wiring problem
* TCM issue

---

## P0720

Description:
Output Speed Sensor Circuit Malfunction

Possible Causes:

* Failed sensor
* Damaged wiring

---

* Valve body issues
