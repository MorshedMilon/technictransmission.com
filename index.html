About Us

Welcome to Technic Automatic Transmission Service—your trusted partner in automatic transmission excellence. Founded and led by John Garofalo, Technic was born from his early passion for automotive excellence. In 1981, he transformed that passion into a thriving business dedicated to providing top-tier automatic transmission services.

For over four decades, we have built our reputation on a foundation of integrity, precision, and unmatched expertise. Our commitment to delivering reliable, high-quality transmission repair and maintenance ensures that every vehicle we service operates smoothly and efficiently, giving our customers peace of mind on the road.

At Technic, we don’t just fix transmissions—we build lasting relationships based on trust, performance, and customer satisfaction.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expertise You Can Trust: Our highly skilled technicians bring years of experience to every job.
  • Customer-Centric Approach: We believe in transparent communication and personalized service.
  • Commitment to Quality: Only the highest standards in parts and workmanship.
